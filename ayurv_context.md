## Evidence Grading System
- **A** = Multiple human RCTs or meta-analysis (robust)
- **B** = Small clinical trials with positive results (promising)
- **C** = Animal studies, in-vitro, or observational data
- **D** = Traditional use only, no modern clinical validation
